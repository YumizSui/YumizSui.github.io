Our group, the Ohue Laboratory at Institute of Science Tokyo, was featured on the FM radio program "Maxell meets College Knowledge". I appeared on the show together with Apakorn Kengkanna, a fellow D3 student. The program is broadcast across FM OSAKA, TOKYO FM, and FM AICHI.

I spoke with the host, singer-songwriter Ami Sakaguchi, about my research, and about [our recent win at the international compound-property prediction competition EUOS25 Challenge](/en/blog/euos25-challenge).

The program airs every Sunday in May from 13:55 to 14:00 JST, and is also available afterwards via [radiko](https://radiko.jp/podcast/channels/8ba45a5f-e577-4365-a19d-b1db86bacab2) time-free. See [FM OSAKA](https://www.fmosaka.net/_ct/17835708) for program details.
